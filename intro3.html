From the hyperlinked text of 12-blue to the life-altering decisions of Bandersnatch, each of these five pieces displays the various ways interactivity can be used to enhance the meaning of the piece. Aside from forcing user engagement, interactivity can add to the meaning of the piece, as in Inanimate Alice, create a feeling of emotional responsibility, as in Bandersnatch, or pique the curiosity of the readers, as in Twelve Blue. Despite the relative lack of control present in all of these pieces, interactivity proves itself a potent tool for electronic storytellers.
